Learn Direct

At learndirect Salisbury we offer FREE qualifications in a range of subjects for employed and unemployed individuals. Whether you are looking to improve your skills for progression at work, or would like to gain qualifications in order to return to work, learndirect can help you.
Working closely with local and national Employers, we aim to create career opportunities for our client base to assist with Jobs, Apprenticeships and work experience.
Learndirect aims to provide an excellent customer-focused learning experience using highly qualified staff, delivered in a friendly and professional environment. We offer educational and employability related qualifications and assistance. Courses include IT, Customer Service, English and Maths, Warehousing, Hospitality, Retail and Health & Social Care.
If you are job seeking and need help with your Career Journey, learndirect can give you all the skills and tools required to apply for any suitable job and present your application in the best possible way.
