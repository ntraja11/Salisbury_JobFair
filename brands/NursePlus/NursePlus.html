Looking to start a career in care? or looking for a new agency?
Nurse Plus is a local nursing agency recruiting full or part time Community Carers, Healthcare Assistants, Support Workers and Nurses. In return for your hard work and excellent client care we offer flexible working solutions, award winning free training plus assistance to gain QCF qualifications, excellent pay rates, pension contribution and holiday pay.
