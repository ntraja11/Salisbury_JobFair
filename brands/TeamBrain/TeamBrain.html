Team Brain Injury Support

The aim of Team Brain Injury Support is to raise the standards in support work provision for clients who have an acquired brain injury.
All support workers are supported by our comprehensive and ongoing dedication to educating our support workers.

We provide support for individuals with a range of support needs from 4 hour visits to 24/7 support. The role of the support worker in the past has been undervalued financially; our pay scales are reflective of the demanding roles that they undertake.
