Backline Logistic Support Services

Backline Logistic Support Services is the West Country’s premier service provider of driving and industrial staff.

From roots as a temporary staffing provider Backline has developed into a one-stop shop offering applicants temporary and permanent recruitment solutions, a wide range of training courses via its accredited training centre.

We offer candidates access to 1000s of positions via our job match scheme, free training courses, return to work programmes as well as a bespoke CV writing service. In addition, we operate a licence upgrade curriculum and offer permanent positions of employment on full time contacts working directly for Backline.

If this is something you would like to discuss please come along and discuss with our team – we look forward to meeting you!
