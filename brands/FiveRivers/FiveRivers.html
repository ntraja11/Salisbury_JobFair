Five Rivers Child Care

Five Rivers Child Care is a leading independent social enterprise working with ‘looked-after children’ who are in need of specialist care, education and therapeutic programmes throughout England and Ireland. We promote a strong, pragmatic philosophy, shared by all of our professional and experienced staff, that each young person is an individual in their own right, with their own unique reactions to trauma. Therefore, we provide bespoke needs-led placement programmes for each young person. We have offered children, staff and foster carers high-impact sector leading support for the last 25 years, guiding them through challenges and difficulties across our specialist services.
