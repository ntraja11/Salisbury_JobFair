Mears Care

Mears Care provide care and support services to vulnerable adults in the community. Our Care Workers help the Service Users to live as independently as possible whilst providing the highest standards of care and support services.
Mears Care currently hold the Help To Live At Home Contract in partnership with Wiltshire Council.
Mears Care are currently recruiting Care Workers in the local area. Mears offer £9.60 p/h plus Milage. Previous experience is preferred but not essential as full paid training will be given to any successful candidate.
There are both full and part time positions available. Mears Care provide Care and Support services 365 days a year across Wiltshire and start caring for the Service Users at 7am and Care continues until 10pm.
