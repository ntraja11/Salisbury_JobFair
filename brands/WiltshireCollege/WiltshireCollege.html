Wiltshire College

The Wiltshire College Work Skills Team provide professional training and support for job-seeking adults in Wiltshire.
Working closely with Job Centre Plus and other local providers and employers, the Work Skills team run accredited courses that can prepare and help you with your job seeking.

We can support you if you are:

  • Unemployed and claiming JSA/ESA benefit
  • Living in Wiltshire
  • 19 years old or over and are eligible to work in the UK


We can help you with:

  • Free pre-employment courses
  • Producing/enhancing your CV
  • Job applications and interview preparation
  • Careers advice
  • Confidence building
