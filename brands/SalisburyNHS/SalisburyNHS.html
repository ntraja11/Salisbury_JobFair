Salisbury NHS Foundation Trust

At Salisbury District Hospital, Salisbury NHS Foundation Trust provides wide range of clinical care, including general acute and emergency services, to approximately 225,000 people in Wiltshire, Dorset and Hampshire.  Specialist services, such as burns, plastic surgery, cleft lip and palate, genetics and rehabilitation, extend to a much wider population of more than three million people.  Our Duke of Cornwall Spinal Treatment Centre covers most of southern England with a population of approximately 11 million people.  Trust staff provide outpatient clinics in other locations in Dorset and Hampshire.  Specialist staff hold outreach clinics in hospitals within the Wessex area and, in total, the Trust employed 3,952 staff at 31 March 2014.  This includes full and part-time staff.
We employ a wide variety of staff from Medical Staffing, Nursing, Midwifery, Scientists, Radiographers, Pharmacists, Therapists through to Housekeeping and Catering.  To find out what vacancies we have visit www.salisbury.nhs.uk/AboutUs/Recruitment or email work@salisbury.nhs.uk
